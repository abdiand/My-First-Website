Little Haiti Welcomes You

Located in the Suburbs of Miami Dade lies a town called Little Haiti. This great community of people are very welcoming to everyone that dares a visit, with roughly 3,456 sq miles.

Now this town might be small, but it is well known for their culinary experience, colorful architecture and outstanding black community.

Food

Now Who doesn't love a food? I personally know a good friend of mine named Biggie loves a nice meal every so often.

Fresh Mangoes, goat, pig's feet, fried pork,ox tail,plantains and mushrooms, the true Creole experience.
